Get to know Mac.

Getting started

Easy to use. Easy to love.

Familiar as iPhone. You’ll feel right at home on Mac, with many of the same apps as iPhone — like Messages, Photos, Notes and FaceTime

Fully loaded. Get started with apps you’ll use every day, like Mail, Calendar and Safari for surfing the web. And if you want to create documents, spreadsheets or presentations, there are powerful built-in productivity apps like Pages, Numbers and Keynote.

Simple setup. Migration Assistant makes it easy to transfer your data — including documents, photos, settings and user accounts — from your previous Mac or PC.

Performance and Battery Life

Go fast. Go far.

Supercharged by Apple silicon. Apple silicon brings game-changing power and speed to Mac. It integrates the CPU, GPU and Neural Engine onto a single power-efficient chip, accelerating everything you do.

Best-in-class battery life. Thanks to the efficiency of Apple silicon, Mac laptops deliver exceptional battery life with incredible performance whether you’re plugged in or not.

Work smart. And fast. From designing a stunning presentation to making an epic spreadsheet, Mac lets you get more done faster — whether you’re running to class or running a business.

Create at the speed of your imagination. From recording a podcast to editing your first feature film, you can do, make and create in all-new ways with the speed and power of the ultra-capable Mac.

Mac and iPhone

Dream team.

Mac answers the call. You can answer calls or messages from your iPhone directly on your Mac. All the messages that appear on your iPhone appear on your Mac, too.

Shoot on iPhone. See it on Mac. Use your iPhone to take a picture or scan a document and have it automatically appear on your Mac.

Start here. Finish there. Start an email on your iPhone and finish it on your Mac. With Handoff, when your Mac and iPhone are near each other, you can seamlessly pass what you’re doing from one device to the other.

Compatibility

Mac runs your favourite apps.

The apps you love. Right on your Mac. From Microsoft 365 to Adobe Creative Cloud to Zoom, all your go-to apps are superfast and snappy on Mac.

Well connected. Mac comes with all the ports you need to use your favourite accessories, like external displays, hard drives, cameras and printers.

Privacy and Security

Your business is nobody else’s.

You are your own password. Touch ID keeps all your passwords at the tip of your finger. So you can use it to unlock your Mac, access passkeys and more.

One app to find it all. If you can’t find your Mac or other Apple devices, the Find My app makes it easy to pinpoint where they are. You can even play a sound to help you locate them.

Designed to help protect your privacy. Mac gives you the freedom to choose what you share and how you share it, so you can use apps more securely, protect your data and keep yourself safe on the web.

Durability

Built to stand the test of time.

Super-duper durable. We design our products with durability in mind. Made with recycled aluminium enclosures, Mac computers are exceptionally well built and ready for pretty much anything.

Reliably up to date. Our free macOS updates deliver the latest features and help keep Mac running smoothly and securely.

Apple Values

Our values drive everything we do.

For a better future. We’re transforming how we make, assemble and recycle Mac. We now use 100% recycled aluminium in the enclosures of Mac mini, MacBook Air and MacBook Pro. Because the Earth won’t wait. And neither will we.

Good for you. Good for the planet. With Apple Trade In, you can get a great value for your current Mac and apply it toward a new device. If your Mac isn’t eligible for credit, we’ll recycle it for free.

Accessibility for all. With customisable Display Settings, Personal Voice and more, Mac has industry-leading built-in accessibility features that help you connect, create and do what you love — in the ways that work best for you.

Get to know iPad.

Productivity

Your workplace can be any place.

Versatility comes standard. There’s never been a more joyful way of getting stuff done than with iPad. Designed for Multi-Touch, simple gestures help you get around smoothly and quickly. Add an Apple Pencil to write and draw your ideas or attach a keyboard to rip through your inbox. And do it all with the game-changing performance of Apple silicon.

Work from anywhere. Connect with family, friends and your team wherever you go. Speedy Wi-Fi lets you download, upload and transfer whatever documents, folders and files you need from just about anywhere. And with 5G, you can stay connected even when you’re away from Wi-Fi.

Store what’s important. Get to it with ease. Organise, share and get to all your stuff with the Files app. And easily access and manage what’s on your iPad, in iCloud or on cloud services like Box.

Creativity

Take your inner artist out and about.

Your most creative canvas yet. With a fully immersive touchscreen, iPad is a canvas that’s ready for you to express your most creative self. From 3D sculpting and composing a beat to painting a masterpiece in Adobe Fresco, iPad is where your most creative ideas come to life.

Apple Pencil is the ultimate creative tool. Apple Pencil lets you easily sketch a new idea in Notes, finish a watercolour in Procreate or draw up production-ready architectural plans in SketchUp.

Take your best shot. And video. iPad has powerful front and back cameras that allow you to create feature films, high-quality photos and other amazing content. Apps like Final Cut Pro and Adobe Lightroom let you shoot, edit and share — all from one portable device.

Learning

Your classroom can be anywhere.

Learning untethered. With fast Wi-Fi and cellular capabilities, built-in cameras help you create powerful connections wherever you are. Join class, attend a lecture on Zoom, tackle a group project on FaceTime or simply stay in touch with colleagues and classmates from all over.

Enhance your education with accessories. Jump-start your notes and projects with accessories that were made for iPad.1 Grab an Apple Pencil and take handwritten and digital notes or connect a keyboard and make quick work of all your emails, essays and presentations.

Apps for discovery and exploration. iPad is perfect for learning something new. From step-by-step instructions with Kitchen Stories to zipping through a self‑guided course, there’s an app for whatever — and however — you want to learn, including immersive augmented reality apps.

Entertainment

Kick back. Tune in. Game on.

Always have your favourite content with you. iPad is a 24/7 all-access pass to your favourite movies, TV shows, books and music wherever you are.

A home theatre in your hands. With an immersive display, amazing speakers and advanced display technologies like ProMotion, all your content looks and sounds amazing.

Ready, set, game. The power of Apple silicon combined with a stunning display makes iPad the ultimate immersive gaming experience. Level up by attaching a keyboard or mouse. You can even connect a controller to create a portable console and play games like Call of Duty®:Warzone™ Mobile.

Apple Pencil

Dream it up. Jot it down.

Apple Pencil sets the standard for drawing, writing and note-taking. Keep a journal, quickly jot down a grocery list and take amazing class notes. Want to convert those notes to text? No problem. iPad is ready to capture every thought.

Calling all artists. Whether it’s designing something beautiful in Procreate, brushing up your photos in Affinity Photo 2 or finishing your home project in Shapr3D, Apple Pencil has the ability to bring your creative ideas to life with incredible precision.

Special features to help your workflow. With iPadOS, Apple Pencil feels more like writing on paper. Easily sign documents, mark up screenshots, write in text fields and even leave a note over an app or screen with Quick Notes.

iPadOS + Apps

Everyday superpowers. Built right in.

Touch. And go. Designed for the large Multi-Touch display, iPadOS is the operating system that makes iPad so powerful and easy to use. It pushes the capabilities of iPad further — all using the power of touch.

Powerful and capable from the start. Essential Apple-designed apps like Keynote, Numbers, Pages and more were created to take full advantage of the unique power and versatility of iPad.

Over a million other ways to use it. Turn your iPad into almost anything — from a maths classroom or workstation to a professional recording studio — with apps from the App Store. With over a million apps made just for iPad, there’s something for everyone.

Beware of counterfeit parts

Some counterfeit and third-party power adapters and batteries may not be designed properly and could
result in safety issues. To ensure you receive a genuine Apple battery during a battery replacement, we
recommend visiting an Apple Authorised Service Provider. If you need a replacement adapter to charge
your Apple device, we recommend getting an Apple power adapter.

Also non-genuine replacement displays may have compromised visual quality and may fail to work
correctly. Apple-certified screen repairs are performed by trusted experts who use genuine Apple parts.
